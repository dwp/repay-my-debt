Scenario rules

DD setup

  • No low payment plan on initial setup – any other amount is fine
  • Enter bank details
  • Payment day between day 10 - day 20
  • Once setup is finished – Return to prototype scenarios

Login to manage plan

  • Make an additional payment first – note: data won't update on the dashboard after payment
  • Manage payment plan
  • Change DD amount to £50.00
  • Optional changes for payment day – if changed choose day 20 - 29
  • Optional changes for bank details
  • Complete flow until plan is updated
  • Back to dashboard
  • End flow – Return to prototype scenarios
