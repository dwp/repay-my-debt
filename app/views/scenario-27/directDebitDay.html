Step 4 of 5 What day of the month do you want your Direct Debit to be taken?

You cannot set up a Direct Debit between the 29th and 31st of the month.

If your chosen day falls on a weekend or bank holiday, your payment will be taken on the next working day.

For example, enter 02 for the second day of the month
