If you want to set up a Direct Debit online:

  • you must be an account holder and be able to authorise Direct Debits with
    your bank or building society
  • you will need your bank or building society account details
  • you will also need your sort code and account number if you have a building society account

{% if data['paymentChoices']=='lowPayments'%} Step 4 of 5 {% else %} Step 3 of 4 {% endif %} How flexible payments work

Flexible payments work in the following way:

  • your chosen payment amount of £{{ data['affordable-amount'] }} will become your minimum monthly target
  • you can make multiple payments in a month to either meet or pay more than your target – when you pay is up to you
  • you can make low payments during difficult times but regular low payments mean we may contact you to discuss your situation
  • you can contact us for help if you're struggling to pay

You need to make a payment today to start your plan so have your card ready – credit card payments will incur extra charges, debit card payments will not.
