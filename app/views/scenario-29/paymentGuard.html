You need to make a payment today to start your plan so have your card ready – credit card payments will incur extra charges, debit card payments will not.

How flexible payments work

  • Your chosen payment amount will become your monthly target
  • You must sign in to your account each month and make payments
  • You can make multiple payments in a month towards your target
  • When you pay is up to you but you must meet your monthly target

If your financial situation changes and you're struggling to keep up with your payments, you can change them at anytime by signing in and managing your plan.
