How the review works

We will send you a letter with details of when your review will take place and instructions to sign in to your account. You will then have 4 weeks to do your review.

During the review, we will ask you questions to understand how much you can afford to pay and help you set up a new payment plan which works for you.

If you are able to increase your plan within the next 12 months, a review may not be needed.
