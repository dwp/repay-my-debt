Can I get another Budgeting Advance?

You can get another Budgeting Advance if you have:

  • been getting Universal Credit, income-related Employment and Support Allowance, Income and Support, income-related Jobseeker's Allowance or State Pension Credit for 6 months or more, unless you need the money to help you start a new job or keep an existing job
  • earned less than £2,600 (£3,600 jointly for couples) in the past 6 months
  • paid off any previous Budgeting Advances

If you're not sure about any of this information, you should contact Universal Credit or refer to your journal.

  • Monday to Friday, 10am to 4pm (excluding public holidays)
