How a payment plan review works

This is a discussion about your financial situation where we will:

  • contact you via text or email a week before your review date to let you know when it will take place
  • Call you on your review date to see how much you can afford to pay based on your finances over the past 12 months
  • set up a new payment plan
  • let you know if a further review will be needed

Once your plan is set up, you can sign in to choose a higher payment plan or pay additional amounts.

If you are able to increase your plan, a review may not be needed.
