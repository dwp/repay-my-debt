If you need help, contact Debt Management on:

  • Telephone: 0800 916 0647
  • Monday to Friday, 10am to 4pm (except public holidays)

Find out about call charges - https://www.gov.uk/call-charges

If you don’t do anything, we will either:

  • contact your employer, where you have one, to make deductions from your pay
  • ask a debt collection agency to collect this money for us

The amount we ask your employer to deduct will depend on what you earn and could be up to 20% of your earnings. However, if you have been prosecuted for a benefit fraud offence, this could rise to 40%.

Your employer may also charge you an administration fee; this will not exceed £1 for each payment deducted from your earnings.

For help and support on dealing with debt and budgeting, contact:

  • Citizens Advice - www.citizensadvice.org.uk
  • StepChange Debt Charity - www.stepchange.org
  • Money Advice Service – www.moneyadviceservice.org.uk
