How a flexible payment plan works

This is not a Direct Debit – you must sign in each month and make at least one payment.

  • Set a target amount to aim for each month to help clear your debt
  • You will need to make a payment today – this will start your plan
  • Sign up for helpful payment reminders
  • Your plan will be reviewed often – if the total amount you pay each month is regularly too low, we may contact you

Set up a Direct Debit

If you want to set up a Direct Debit online:

  • you must be an account holder and be able to authorise Direct Debits with
    your bank or building society
  • you will need your bank or building society account details
  • you will also need your sort code and account number if you have a building society account

Once your plan is setup, you can sign in to manage your plan to track payments,
pay additional amounts or get help and support.

I cannot set up a Direct Debit online

If you have a joint account and you and your partner both need to authorise
Direct Debits, you can set up a Direct Debit by downloading and completing
a Direct Debit mandate:
