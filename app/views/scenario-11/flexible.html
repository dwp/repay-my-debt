How flexible payments work

  1. Pay what you can afford
  2. This is not a fixed plan and your payments can change each month
  3. Make as many payments as you like but you must make at least one payment per month
  4. Sign up for helpful payment reminders via text or email
  5. If you do not keep up with your payments, we may ask your employer to make deductions from your pay or ask a debt collection agency to collect this money for us
